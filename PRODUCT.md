# Product

## Register

product

## Users

MeuBills is for one primary user managing personal and professional finances across separate financial contexts, such as PF, PJ, investments, crypto, and foreign holdings. The user works on web and mobile, often from a PWA on a phone, and needs the product to remain useful while offline.

The user wants Mobills-like familiarity for daily financial control, including a similar dark dashboard feel, colorful summary cards, and direct operational flows, but with stronger separation between financial groups and a clearer view of multi-currency net worth across BRL, USD, and BTC.

The mobile app should be especially familiar to an existing Mobills user. The home layout may closely follow the Mobills mental model: month selector, current balance hero, income and expense pair, pending alerts, credit-card invoices, bottom navigation, and central add action.

## Product Purpose

MeuBills helps the user record transactions, manage accounts and credit cards, review operational reports, and understand consolidated net worth across currencies and financial groups.

Success means the user can trust the numbers, register daily movements quickly, operate offline without anxiety, and understand how their total financial position changes over time.

The app UI language is Portuguese Brazilian (pt-BR). Dates, currency formatting, labels, validation messages, empty states, and financial copy should use Brazilian conventions.

## Brand Personality

Focused, vivid, and trustworthy.

The product should feel like a serious financial tool for daily use with the energy of a Mobills-style dashboard: colorful, fast to scan, and motivating without becoming noisy. Its tone is direct and reassuring, with labels and states that make money movement easy to verify.

## Anti-references

- Do not use Mobills logos, proprietary assets, brand marks, or exact copyrighted materials.
- A Mobills-like product feel is acceptable: dark financial dashboard, colorful metric cards, compact sidebar/bottom navigation, charts, and credit-card panels.
- The mobile home may closely copy the arrangement of familiar Mobills elements, while using original implementation and MeuBills-specific data, groups, and assets.
- Avoid bank-like navy and gold, corporate fintech gloss, neon crypto aesthetics, and oversized marketing dashboard cards.
- Avoid hiding core workflows behind playful visuals or decorative motion.
- Avoid a ledger/accounting interface that feels hostile to non-accountants.

## Design Principles

- Put trust before flourish. Financial data must be legible, traceable, and hard to misread.
- Separate worlds clearly. PF, PJ, investments, crypto, and foreign accounts should not blur together unless the user asks for consolidation.
- Give each world a visual identity. Groups such as PF, PJ, Dolar, and Bitcoin should be recognizable at a glance without overpowering financial semantics.
- Make multi-currency net worth the signature. Operational control matters, but BRL, USD, and BTC visibility is the differentiator.
- Keep the daily path short. Recording, reviewing, and correcting financial data should be fast on mobile.
- Design for offline honesty. The interface should say what is local, what is synced, and what may be stale.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG AA for contrast, focus states, keyboard navigation, touch target sizing, and readable text. Support reduced motion. Do not rely on color alone for financial states, sync status, account groups, or currency distinctions.

Use pt-BR language metadata in the app shell and keep copy clear for Brazilian financial vocabulary.
